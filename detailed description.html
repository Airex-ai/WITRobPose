Background of WITRobPose:
        In recent years, there are more and more application scenarios of industrial robots, such as light industry, warehousing logistics and agriculture. According to a survey, 94% of Companies in the UK and the US say they have accepted robots as indispensable in the future. A survey of OSHA accident reports shows that there have been 38 robot related accidents since 1984. Of the 38 incidents, 27 resulted in the death of workers. According to the Bureau of labor, 4585 workers died in accidents involving robots in the United States. These accidents are all related to industrial robots, most of which occur during maintenance, when workers have to enter the fence for unit testing and troubleshooting. In fact, these tragedies happened in the process of making cars by large robots can be avoided. Therefore, in order to solve the problem of industrial robot hurting people from the root, we take the working principle of industrial robot into account, starting from the attitude of industrial robot, mark the key nodes of industrial robot and obtain its attitude to monitor the working state of industrial robot. In case of misoperation, corresponding measures can be implemented immediately.
Purpose and significance of WITRobPose:
        In order to ensure the personal health and safety of workers, it is necessary to recognize the attitude of industrial robots. This can not only provide data for solving the current problem of industrial robots hurting people, but also provide resources for other problems of industrial robots.
